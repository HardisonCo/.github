## HMS Implementation Status

### Full HMS Implementation
The following agencies have fully implemented the HMS architecture:
- Department of Health and Human Services (HHS)
- Department of Justice (DOJ)
- Department of Energy (DOE)
- Department of Education (ED)
- Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

### HMS Implementation In Progress
The following agencies are currently implementing the HMS architecture:
- Department of Agriculture (USDA)
- Department of Homeland Security (DHS)
- Department of Defense (DOD)
- Department of Veterans Affairs (VA)
- Social Security Administration (SSA)
- Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)

### HMS Planning Phase
The following agencies are in the planning phase for HMS implementation:
- Department of Commerce (DOC)
- Department of Labor (DOL)
- Department of Transportation (DOT)
- Small Business Administration (SBA)
- Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC)
- Federal Reserve System
