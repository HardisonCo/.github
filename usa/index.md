# United States Government Agencies

This documentation provides comprehensive information about HMS integration with United States government agencies at the federal and state levels.

## Government Levels

- [Federal Agencies](Federal/index.md) - US Federal Government Agencies
- [State Agencies](State/index.md) - US State Government Agencies

## HMS Components Overview

The HMS (Hardison Management System) architecture consists of multiple components that can be integrated with government agencies at various levels. Each component provides specific functionality within the system.

For more information about each component and its integration with specific agencies, please navigate to the relevant agency documentation.
