# Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (HMS-NFO Integration)

## Agency Overview

Protects the retirement incomes of American workers by insuring defined-benefit pension plans nationwide.

**Core Function:** Retirement benefit promise backstop

## First Principles Analysis

The PBGC exists to ensure continuation of basic pension benefits despite employer inability to fulfill pension obligations. Its fundamental purpose is providing insurance that protects retirement income when pension plans terminate without sufficient assets. The agency operates on the principle that retirement security requires protection against both individual longevity risk and plan failure risk. Its functions include collecting premiums, monitoring plan funding, and assuming responsibility for insolvent plans, recognizing that effective pension insurance requires both financial resources to pay benefits and regulatory authority to minimize insurance losses.

### Fundamental Principles

- Long-term pension obligations create inherent funding vulnerabilities
- Corporate bankruptcies threaten retirement security without safety net
- Defined benefit promises span decades, requiring institutional continuity
- Retirement security affects broader economic stability and social welfare

### Key Mechanisms

- Insure defined benefit pension plans up to specific limits
- Collect premiums from pension sponsors to fund insurance
- Assume responsibility for terminated underfunded pension plans
- Manage investments to pay future benefit obligations

## HMS-NFO Integration

The HMS-NFO system provides specialized data and analytics services tailored to Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation's mission. The following components are particularly relevant:

- **Internet Data Collection System**: Gathers trade and economic data from public sources
- **Data Access API**: Provides standardized access to HMS-NFO data
